Personal Info

My name is Peter G. Morales, and I am a Silicon Alley software developer!

Before I decided to change careers, I was working as an electrician. While I was in trade school,
I learned about something called, "Ladder Logic". What I found out (through research on my own)
is Ladder Logic is a type of programming language.

Unlike C (or most other language), it's graphical, rather than textual. If you're familiar with
schematics or blueprints, it's kind-of like that. Along with some other aspects I learned, such
as where the construction industry was going, I decided to fully pursue a career in software
development. My short-term goal is to become competent in web development technologies. My
long-term goal is to become proficient in app technologies that work with Apple.

What I learned in the trade school inspired me to research into software development. This
gave me the time to find out if this was "something I could handle". The connection I made
between electrical engineering and computer science made the learning of programming easier,
I think. And it gave me the confidence, and the hope, I needed.
